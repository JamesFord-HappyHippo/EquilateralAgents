Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

At Equilateral AI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our AI agent orchestration services, visit our site, or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you contact us or submit content, we may collect:

  • Email address and name when you contact us
  • Project requirements and technical specifications for agent services
  • Feedback, questions, or comments you send us

Website Analytics

We use standard web analytics to understand how our site is used:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • General location information (country/region)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referring websites and search terms

Note: We use privacy-focused analytics and do not track you across other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Communication: To respond to your emails and questions
  • Service Delivery: To provide AI agent orchestration and development acceleration services
  • Site Improvement: To understand how our content is used and improve the experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your information for:

  • Selling or renting your information to third parties
  • Targeted advertising or marketing
  • Building detailed profiles for commercial purposes

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your story or content
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted providers who help us operate the site (hosting, analytics)

Client Projects

For enterprise AI agent services, we may:

  • Process your codebase and project data to provide pattern harvesting services
  • Store project configurations and deployment settings securely
  • Use anonymized performance metrics for service improvement
  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all client intellectual property

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Secure hosting with encrypted data transmission
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Prompt notification if any security incidents occur

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to following industry best practices.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@equilateral.ai.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: For basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand site usage (privacy-focused)

We do not use:

  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking technologies

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any websites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our AI agent orchestration services are intended for enterprise software development teams and technology professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may notify you via email if we have your contact information.
